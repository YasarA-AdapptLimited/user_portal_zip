Application to sit the registration assessment

Registration assessment eligibility

In order to apply to sit the upcoming registration assessment you need to submit this application, pay the application fee by the advertised deadline and confirm that you have read and understood the registration assessment regulations.

Fee payment

The payment that you make will only be applicable for the upcoming registration assessment. If you withdraw from the registration assessment you will receive a refund in due course. In order to sit a subsequent registration assessment you will need to apply again once the application has been made available and pay the required fee.

Confirmation of application

Once you have paid the application fee you will receive a notification that your registration assessment application is being processed.

We will issue a confirmation of your final entry approximately one week after the application deadline. If you do not receive this notification within 10 days of the assessment application deadline, please contact regexam@pharmacyregulation.org.

Contact details

Please keep your contact information up to date within myGPhC.

Assessment results are issued via myGPhC,you should ensure you have agreed to receive GPhC emails to get a notification when your results are released.
