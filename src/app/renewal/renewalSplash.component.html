Direct Debit single quarterly payment

You have a direct debit in place so you will not have to make a card payment to process your renewal.

Please see your renewal notice to find out when your Direct Debit payment will be taken. Your receipt will be available to download after your Direct Debit payment has been taken. the first Direct Debit payment has been taken. The amount paid and amount still owing displayed on this receipt will be updated after each payment is taken.

Card payments

We can only accept payments made in pounds sterling (GBP).

When you make a card payment, you may be redirected to your card provider’s site to enter another password before they authorise your payment. You can find out more about 3-D Secure authentication from your card provider.

Receipts

You can view and print receipts for your renewal payments by going to the receipts page.

Refund Policy

We do not give refunds of renewal fee payments unless we have made a mistake in processing your payment.
