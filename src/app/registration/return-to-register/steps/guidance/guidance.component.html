Use this application to apply to return to the register. It will be available to submit for 12 months from the date you were last registered.

After this time, you will need to complete a return to practise application, which you can find on the main GPhC website.

You must pay two fees as part of the application. The first is an application fee, the amount depends on the way you left the register and if you are a pharmacist or pharmacy technician. You can see the amounts in the tables below.

The application fee covers the processing of your application and is non-refundable, even if your application is not successful.

The second is the restoration fee which covers your registration until you reach the next anniversary of your previous renewal deadline. Then you will need to pay for your next year's registration as normal.

If you are returning to the register in the two months before the anniversary of your previous renewal deadline, you will also need to pay the renewal fee for the following year at the same time as the restoration fee.
