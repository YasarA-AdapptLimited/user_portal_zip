Confirmation of approval to sit the registration assessment

This letter confirms that you are approved to sit the upcoming assessment. It sets out the steps you must take to arrange your assessment sitting, and key information about the assessment process. Please make sure you read it carefully and follow the instructions.

What happens next?

Your test centre place

You will be allocated a place to sit at the closest available centre to you, based on the post code of the address we hold for you in your myGPhC account.

You will be able to view details of your test centre place on the Scheduler system.

You will have the opportunity to change test centre if you want to for a short time, subject to availability.

Managing your test centre place

You will receive a welcome email from Scheduler 48 hours before you will have access to Scheduler, the test centre portal. The email will confirm your account has been created and give you instructions on when you will be able to see your booking and how to change your test centre place, if you want to.

Once you have access to your account, you will be able to change your test centre place if you want to, subject to availability.

For more information on test centre places and how to change your booking, see the foundation trainee page where you can find the dedicated web page for the upcoming sitting. More information on key dates and timelines can also be found through the trainee page.

More information about sitting the assessment

Make sure you have read, and are aware of, all the information on the assessment webpage and, including key dates. You will be able to find more information on:

  • what to expect on the day of the assessment
  • making the decision that you are ‘fit to sit’
  • how you will receive your results

We strongly recommend you read the guide to sitting the assessment in the ‘On the day’ section of the webpage, as this provides you with the necessary information for before, during and after the assessment.

If you have any questions which are not answered in this letter, or on the assessment webpage, please email regexam@pharmacyregulation.org.

We would like to take this opportunity to wish you success with your registration assessment sitting.
