Once you have finished your foundation training successfully and met all the other registration requirements, you will be eligible to apply for registration as a pharmacist.

Once you are registered you will receive a “Notice of entry” letter via myGPhC confirming this. The letter will include your GPhC registration number. From the day you are registered, you will also be able to see your registration on the live copy of the GPhC’s register of pharmacists at pharmacyregulation.org.

Until you have your registration number you must not work as a pharmacist or call yourself one.

On behalf of the GPhC, we would like to wish you all the best for your future career.
