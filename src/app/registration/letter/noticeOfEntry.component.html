I am pleased to confirm that your application has been successful and your name has been entered in part 1 part 2 of the General Pharmaceutical Council register. Under the Pharmacy Order 2010 your entry in the register is valid for one calendar year from the date of entry, and you must renew your entry at least two months before it expires.

All pharmacy professionals on our register must keep to the GPhC registration rules and the standards for pharmacy professionals, which help to make sure people using pharmacy services receive safe and effective care. This includes carrying regular professional development as set out in the GPhC revalidation framework. You can find out more about these requirements on our website, www.pharmacyregulation.org.
