Dear {{ registrantName }},

Thank you for your application to voluntarily remove your name from the General Pharmaceutical Council's register. I write to confirm that your name has been removed from the register.

If you want to re-join the register within 12 months of your removal date, you can apply to restore your entry to the register, the application can be accessed when you open your myGPhC account.

If in the future you want to re-join the register after 12 months has passed, you would need to apply to return to the register. You can find more information about restoring your register entry and about returning to the register on our website.

Once you leave the register, you will still be able to access and update your myGPhC account for two years after your leaving date. After this time, your entire account (including any revalidation records) will be deleted.

Should you have any queries please contact our customer services team on 0203 713 8000 or email info@pharmacyregulation.org.

Thank you for your contribution to pharmacy profession during your time as a registered pharmacy professional. We wish you good luck in the future.
