Use this application to apply to register as a pharmacy technician after you have obtained recognised pharmacy technician qualifications and have completed two years work-based experience in the UK, Isle of Man, or Channel Islands.

Before continuing any further make sure that you have read our full application guidance. If you do not follow the full instructions in the guidance your registration may be delayed.

To apply to register as a pharmacy technician you must submit this online application and post us supporting identity documents. Your application is not complete until you have submitted this online application and we have received your posted supporting documents.
