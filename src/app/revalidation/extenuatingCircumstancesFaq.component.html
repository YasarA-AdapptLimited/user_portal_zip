Telling us about exceptional circumstances

There are sometimes reasons why you will not be able to submit some or all of your revalidation records when you renew your registration – because of serious illness, or maternity leave, for example. It’s important that you tell us as soon as you know that you will not be able to meet the submission requirement.

Use this form to

  • tell us why you are not able to meet the requirements
  • request changes to the requirements
    (usually an extension to the due date, or a submission of fewer entries) which you will be able to meet
  • provide evidence to support your reasons

We will consider your request and contact you to let you know if we are satisfied with your reasons and the changes you have requested, or if we need to discuss the matter further. If you do not receive a response from us within 28 working days of submitting your form, please contact us.

Tell us why you are not able to meet the requirements

Please give a brief explanation of the reasons why you are aren’t able to submit all of you records by your renewal due date. Make sure you explain how your situation has affected your ability to carry out and record CPD. If you have been or are likely to be absent from work, include this too. This will help us understand the reasons for the changes you request.

Request changes to the requirements

There are two main changes you can request the revalidation requirement:

  1. A submission extension You may want to request an extension deadline if you have carried out at least the required number of revalidation activities throughout your registration year, but need more time to write up and submit these records. While the extension is in place, you will still need to carry out and record new revalidation activities and submit these by your next renewal due date.
  2. Submitting fewer records If you have not been, or will not be, working as a pharmacy professional or carrying out revalidation activities for part of your registration year (because of a serious illness, or maternity leave, for example), you may want to request that you submit fewer revalidation records by your current renewal date. Use the form to tell us how many entries you will be able to submit. If there are any gaps in your records like this, they should not normally be longer than 12 months.

Important: If you don’t think you will be able to meet the revalidation requirement even with these changes, please contact us.

Provide evidence to support your reasons

The evidence you provide should help to confirm the reasons you give for not being able to meet the revalidation requirement. You must provide at least one document to support your request – we will not consider a request without any supporting evidence.

The kinds of documents you send to us will depend on the reasons for your request. For example, some of the more common reasons for requesting changes to the requirements and the kinds of documents that you could use to support these are:

  • maternity leave: a copy of your MAT1B form
  • sickness leave: a doctor’s note or other medical documents which explain your condition

You can upload up to four documents. Each file must be smaller than 8MB.

Please see our privacy statement for more information on how we handle the personal information you provide to us.

Important: You must give genuine information and evidence in this request. If we find that you have given false or misleading information, you will be in breach of the standards for pharmacy professionals .
